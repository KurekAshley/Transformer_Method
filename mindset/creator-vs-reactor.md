# Creator vs Reactor

## Status

Core Doctrine  
Transformer Method Canon

---

## Definition

Every person lives from one of two primary states of consciousness:

```text
Creator
```

or

```text
Reactor
```

The Creator consciously influences and shapes reality.

The Reactor allows reality to dictate thoughts, emotions, behaviours, and decisions.

This distinction is one of the most important in the Transformer Method.

It determines whether a person becomes the author of their future or the victim of their circumstances.

---

## Core Principle

Life is not determined by what happens.

Life is determined by how we respond to what happens.

The Creator understands:

```text
I am responsible for my response.
```

The Reactor believes:

```text
My circumstances determine my response.
```

The difference changes everything.

---

## The Fundamental Question

Every challenge presents a choice.

```text
Will I create?

or

Will I react?
```

This choice determines the future.

---

## The Creator Mindset

The Creator asks:

```text
What can I do?

What can I learn?

What can I create?

How can I grow?

How would Love respond?
```

Creators focus on:

- solutions
- responsibility
- possibility
- growth
- learning
- vision
- action

Creators understand that power exists in response.

---

## The Reactor Mindset

The Reactor asks:

```text
Why is this happening to me?

Who is to blame?

Why is life unfair?

Why can't I catch a break?
```

Reactors focus on:

- circumstances
- excuses
- blame
- problems
- limitations
- victimhood
- external control

Reactors surrender power to conditions.

---

## The Formula Of Reaction

Reaction follows a predictable sequence.

```text
Circumstance
↓
Thought
↓
Emotion
↓
Reaction
↓
Result
```

The circumstance becomes the source.

The person becomes an effect.

---

## The Formula Of Creation

Creation follows a different sequence.

```text
Vision
↓
Thought
↓
Emotion
↓
Action
↓
Result
```

The vision becomes the source.

The person becomes the cause.

---

## Cause And Effect

Reactors live at effect.

Creators live at cause.

```text
Creator
↓
Cause
```

```text
Reactor
↓
Effect
```

Creators ask:

```text
What is my contribution?
```

Reactors ask:

```text
Who is responsible?
```

One creates power.

One creates helplessness.

## Responsibility Creates Freedom

Many people misunderstand responsibility.

They hear:

```text
It's my responsibility.
```

and think:

```text
It's my fault.
```

These are not the same.

Responsibility means:

```text
I have the ability to respond.
```

The word itself contains the lesson:

```text
Response-Ability
```

The ability to choose a response.

Examples:


```text

```

```text

```



The event is not the determining factor.

The response is.
