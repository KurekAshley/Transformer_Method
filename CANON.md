# Purpose

This document defines the official canon of the Transformer Method.

Its purpose is to establish a single authoritative reference for what constitutes canonical Transformer Method doctrine.

As the repository grows, new doctrines, frameworks, models, distinctions, diagrams, certifications, assessments, and educational materials may be added.

CANON.md serves as the definitive source that identifies what officially belongs to the Transformer Method.

---

# What Is Canon?

Canon is the official body of knowledge that defines the Transformer Method.

A doctrine becomes canonical when it:

- Aligns with the philosophy of the Transformer Method
- Meets doctrinal standards
- Integrates with existing doctrine
- Demonstrates transformational value
- Receives approval from the repository owner

Canonical content represents the official teachings of the Transformer Method.
