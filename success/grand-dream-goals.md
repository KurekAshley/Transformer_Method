# Grand Dream Goals

## Status

Core Doctrine  
Transformer Method Canon

---

## Definition

A Grand Dream Goal is a goal so large, inspiring, meaningful, and transformational that it requires a person to become someone new in order to achieve it.

A Grand Dream Goal is not designed to get something.

It is designed to become someone.

Most goals focus on achievement.

Grand Dream Goals focus on transformation.

---

## Core Principle

The true purpose of a Grand Dream Goal is not the goal itself.

The true purpose is the person you become while pursuing it.

```text
Grand Dream Goal
↓
Expanded Identity
↓
Expanded Behaviour
↓
Expanded Results
↓
Expanded Life
```

The goal is the vehicle.

Transformation is the destination.

---

## The Great Misunderstanding

Most people set goals they already believe they can achieve.

These goals create progress.

They rarely create transformation.

A Grand Dream Goal is different.

A Grand Dream Goal should be so significant that your current identity is incapable of achieving it.

That is precisely why it is valuable.

---

## Why Grand Dream Goals Matter

A person's life expands in direct proportion to the size of their vision.

Small goals create small growth.

Large goals create large growth.

Grand Dream Goals force:

- learning
- growth

- creativity
- leadership
- faith

The goal becomes a catalyst for evolution.
