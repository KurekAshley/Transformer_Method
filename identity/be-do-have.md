# Be → Do → Have

## Status

Core Doctrine  
Transformer Method Canon

---

## Definition

Most people attempt to create life using the sequence:

```text
Have
↓
Do
↓
Be
```

They believe:

"When I have the money, then I will do what successful people do, and then I will become successful."

"When I have confidence, then I will take action, and then I will become confident."

"When I have the relationship, then I will feel loved."

This is the backwards approach to creation.

The Transformer Method teaches a different sequence:

```text
Be
↓
Do
↓
Have
```

Who you become determines what you do.

What you do determines what you have.

---

## Core Principle

Results are not created by what you have.

Results are created by who you are being.

Being creates doing.

Doing creates having.

Identity creates reality.

---

## The Universal Sequence

Every result follows the same process.

```text
Identity
↓
Being
↓
Action
↓
Results
```

The outside world reflects the inside world.

Change the inside and the outside eventually follows.

## Why Most Goals Fail

Most people focus entirely on outcomes.

They ask:

```text
What do I want to have?
```

Very few ask:

```text
Who must I become?
```

Goals fail because people attempt to create new results with an old identity.

They want a new future while remaining the same person.

---

## The Identity Gap

Every goal requires a corresponding identity.

Example:

A person wants:

```text
A million-dollar business.
```

The question is not:

```text
How do I make a million dollars?
```

The question is:

```text
Who must I become to create a million-dollar business?
```

The greater the goal, the greater the required identity expansion.

---

## The Creator's Question

Most people ask:

```text
What do I need?
```

Creators ask:

```text
Who do I need to become?
```

This question shifts attention from circumstances to transformation.

## The Have → Do → Be Trap

The majority of people unconsciously operate from:

```text
Have
↓
Do
↓
Be
```

Examples:

"If I had more money, I would invest."

"If I had confidence, I would speak."

"If I had support, I would start."

"If I had time, I would change."

This creates permanent delay.

Life becomes conditional.

Growth becomes postponed.

## The Be → Do → Have Path

Creators reverse the sequence.

```text
Be
↓
Do
↓
Have
```

Examples:

Be disciplined.

Do disciplined actions.

Have disciplined results.

Be courageous.

---

Have health.

---

Being always comes first.
