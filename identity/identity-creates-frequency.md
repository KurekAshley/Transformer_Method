# Identity Creates Frequency

## Status

Core Doctrine  
Transformer Method Canon

---

## Definition

Identity is the source of frequency.

Every frequency a person generates originates from who they believe they are.

Most people attempt to change their thoughts.

Others attempt to change their emotions.

Some attempt to change their behaviours.

Yet all of these are downstream effects.

Identity sits at the source.

Change identity and frequency changes naturally.

## Expectation Is The Bridge

Expectation connects identity to frequency.

Identity answers:

```text
Who am I?
```

Expectation answers:

```text
What do I expect?
```

Frequency emerges from the answer.

Example:

```text
Identity:
I am capable.

Expectation:
Things will work out.

Frequency:
Confidence.
```

Or:

```text
Identity:
I always fail.

Expectation:
This won't work.

Frequency:
Fear.
```

Identity determines expectation.

Expectation influences frequency.

## Frequency Cannot Exceed Identity

People can temporarily elevate their emotional state.

They cannot permanently sustain a frequency that exceeds their identity.

Eventually they return to what feels familiar.

This explains why many breakthroughs are temporary.

The frequency changed.

The identity did not.

---

## The Thermostat Principle

Identity functions like a thermostat.

Frequency is the temperature.

If the thermostat is set low:

The room eventually returns to that setting.

Likewise:

If identity remains unchanged:

Frequency eventually returns to familiar patterns.

To permanently elevate frequency:

Raise the identity.
