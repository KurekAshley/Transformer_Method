## Definition

The Formula of Creation explains how all human-created results come into existence.

Every result is first created internally before it appears externally.

Creation always occurs twice:

1. First in the mind.
2. Then in physical reality.

The visible result is the final stage of a process that began long before the result appeared.

Everything that exists was once only a thought.

Every business.
Every relationship.
Every invention.
Every achievement.
Every movement.
Every success.

Someone imagined it before it existed.

Creation is the process of transforming the invisible into the visible.

Most people quit during the invisible phase.

- thoughts have changed
- identity has changed
- behaviour has changed

but results have not yet appeared.

Creation is still occurring.

The seed is growing beneath the soil.

The absence of visible proof is not proof that nothing is happening.
