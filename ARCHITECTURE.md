# Purpose

This document defines the structural architecture of the Transformer Method Canon.

While doctrine explains **what the Transformer Method teaches**, architecture explains **how the knowledge system is organized**.

The purpose of this architecture is to:

- Preserve intellectual property
- Maintain doctrinal consistency
- Enable future expansion
- Support education and certification
- Support books, courses, coaching, AI systems, and licensing
- Create a permanent canonical knowledge framework

---

# Architecture Philosophy

The Transformer Method is not a collection of isolated concepts.

It is an integrated transformational system.

Every doctrine connects to and supports other doctrines.

The architecture exists to ensure that all concepts remain connected to the larger system.

# Expansion Principles

New doctrine must:

1. Align with core philosophy.
2. Support existing doctrine.
3. Maintain terminology standards.
4. Follow canonical writing standards.
5. Be approved through governance procedures.
6. Strengthen the overall architecture.

Expansion must increase clarity rather than complexity.
