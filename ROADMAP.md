# Transformer Method Roadmap

## Purpose

This roadmap outlines the planned development of the Transformer Method Repository.

The purpose of the roadmap is to provide clarity, direction, and strategic focus as the repository evolves into the definitive source for the Transformer Method operating system.

The roadmap identifies major doctrine categories, future framework development, supporting resources, and long-term objectives.

---

# Vision

To create the world's most comprehensive transformational operating system.

A complete body of work that explains:

- How transformation occurs
- How identity is created
- How purpose is discovered
- How frequency influences results
- How success is achieved
- How human potential is expanded

The Transformer Method Repository serves as the foundation for future:

- Books
- Courses
- Workshops
- Coaching Programs
- Certifications
- AI Systems
- Educational Institutions
