# Cells Of Recognition

## Status

Core Doctrine  
Transformer Method Canon

## Definition

Cells of Recognition are the mental, emotional, and perceptual mechanisms that determine what opportunities, possibilities, solutions, and resources become visible to an individual.

People do not experience all of reality.

They experience the portion of reality they are capable of recognizing.

The opportunities available to a person are often far greater than the opportunities they can currently see.

Recognition determines access.

---

## Core Principle

You cannot act upon what you cannot see.

You cannot pursue what you cannot recognize.

You cannot create from possibilities that remain invisible.

The expansion of awareness expands available opportunity.

Reality contains more information than the conscious mind can process.

As a result, the brain filters information.

Only information considered relevant is allowed into conscious awareness.

```text

Awareness
↓

Results
```
