## Status

Core Doctrine

---

## Definition

The Formula is the universal pattern behind all success, transformation, creation, and lasting change.

No person invented it.

It is not Kurek Ashley’s formula, Bob Proctor’s formula, Napoleon Hill’s formula, Wallace D. Wattles’ formula, or Maxwell Maltz’s formula.

They each discovered, studied, explained, or applied the same underlying law from different angles.

The Formula is the Formula.

---

## Core Principle

Successful people and unsuccessful people are not separated by luck, background, intelligence, education, or circumstance.

They are separated by whether they are living in alignment with the Formula.

When the Formula is present, success becomes repeatable.

When the Formula is missing, people struggle, force, chase, react, and often reproduce the same results over and over again.
