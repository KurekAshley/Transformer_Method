## Definition

The Law of Harmonization states that similar frequencies naturally move toward alignment.

Everything in life seeks harmony with its dominant frequency.

People, relationships, environments, organizations, opportunities, habits, and outcomes tend to organize themselves around the frequencies being generated.

The frequency becomes the organizing force.

Harmonization is one of the primary mechanisms through which reality takes form.
