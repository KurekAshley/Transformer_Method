# Weed Killer Thinking

## Status

Core Doctrine  
Transformer Method Canon

---

## Definition

Weed Killer Thinking is the habit of planting a goal, dream, vision, or possibility and then immediately destroying it with contradictory thoughts.

Most people are not limited by a lack of goals.

They are limited by the thinking that follows the goal.

They plant seeds of possibility and then poison them with doubt, fear, worry, criticism, and disbelief.

The result is predictable.

The seed never reaches full expression.

---

## Core Principle

Every thought either nourishes creation or weakens it.

```text
Vision

↓
Frequency
↓
Behaviour
↓
Results
```

Empowering thoughts strengthen the vision.

Disempowering thoughts weaken the vision.

The quality of thinking determines the quality of creation.

New possibilities threaten existing identities.

The goal is not growth.

The goal is familiarity.

This is why transformation requires awareness.

## Replacing Weed Killer Thinking

The goal is not blind optimism.

The goal is intentional thinking.

Replace:

```text

```

with:

```text

```

Replace:

```text

```

with:

```text

```

Replace:

```text
I'm not ready.
```

with:

```text

```

Empowering questions create empowering possibilities.
