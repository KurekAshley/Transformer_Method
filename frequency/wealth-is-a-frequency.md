# Wealth Is A Frequency

## Status

Core Doctrine  
Transformer Method Canon

---

## Definition

Wealth is not merely money.

Wealth is a frequency.

Money is one possible expression of that frequency.

Most people pursue money while ignoring the frequency that creates it.

The Transformer Method teaches that wealth begins internally before it appears externally.

Before wealth appears in form, it exists in frequency.

---

## Core Principle

Money follows value.

Value follows behaviour.

Behaviour follows frequency.

Frequency follows identity.

```text
Identity
↓
Expectation
↓
Frequency
↓
Behaviour
↓
Value Creation
↓
Wealth
```

The source of wealth is not money.

The source of wealth is identity.

---

## The Difference Between Money And Wealth

Money is a resource.

Wealth is a state.

A person can possess money and still operate from scarcity.

A person can have limited money and operate from abundance.

Money is external.

Wealth begins internally.

The internal state eventually influences the external result.
